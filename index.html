// The Lineup

Our AI agents

Eight specialized agents that cover every growth lever for your business. Mix and match based on where you're leaking revenue.

◉
ECHO
AI Receptionist

24/7 voice agent that answers calls, books appointments, and captures every lead.

ECHO is trained on your business details, services, hours, and pricing. It handles common questions, books directly into your calendar, and sends you a summary of every call. Works nights, weekends, and holidays. Transfers to you when something needs a human touch.

⟶
IGNITE
Lead Response Agent

Responds to every inbound lead instantly via email, call, and text.

IGNITE connects to your forms, ads, and inbox. When a new lead comes in, it responds in under 5 seconds with a personalized message. It qualifies the lead, answers basic questions, and books them straight into your calendar. No more leads going cold because you were busy.

↻
REVIVE
Customer Reactivation

Brings back dormant customers with personalized outreach campaigns.

REVIVE scans your customer database for people who haven't visited in 6+ months. It sends personalized texts and emails with offers tailored to their history. Typical campaigns recover 8-12% of dormant customers in the first 30 days.

◎
SCOUT
Lead Generator

Finds, ranks, and contacts your ideal customers at scale.

SCOUT builds targeted prospect lists based on your ideal customer profile, location, and industry. It ranks leads by fit, enriches contact data, and can initiate outreach sequences. Up to 5,000 leads per batch.

◈
FORGE
AI Websites

Conversion-optimized websites researched and built for your business.

FORGE researches your market, competitors, and customers, then builds a site designed to convert visitors into booked appointments. Every page is written for your specific services and pricing. Typically launched within a week.

◬
SIGNAL
AI SEO (GEO)

Makes your business visible to AI search engines and assistants.

SIGNAL optimizes your website structure, content, and metadata so AI engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity recommend your business. When someone asks "best dentist near me," SIGNAL makes sure you show up.

◆
ORACLE
Growth Intelligence

Business intelligence and growth strategy, updated weekly.

ORACLE monitors your market, tracks competitor moves, and delivers weekly strategy briefs with specific action items. It identifies opportunities you're missing and threats you should know about. Think of it as a fractional CMO powered by AI.

≋
FLUX
Custom Automations

Tell us the problem. We build the AI solution.

FLUX handles everything that doesn't fit neatly into another agent. Invoice processing, inventory alerts, review monitoring, staff scheduling, custom integrations. If it can be automated with AI, we build it.
